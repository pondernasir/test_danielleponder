danielle ponder
Musician
“Bravery is finding your voice—and owning it.”
Danielle Ponder is a soulful singer-songwriter from Rochester who made a bold leap from a successful career as a public defender to pursue her true passion: music. Her debut album, Some of Us Are Brave, blends pop, R&B, blues, rock, and trip-hop into a hauntingly original sound, anchored by her powerful, emotive voice. Drawing from a rich musical upbringing and diverse influences, Danielle’s songs explore resilience, love, and personal growth with honesty and depth. Named after a landmark book on Black women’s experiences, the album celebrates bravery in all its forms—reflecting Danielle’s journey from advocacy to artistry and inviting listeners to feel seen, encouraged, and inspired.
